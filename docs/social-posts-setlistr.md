# SetListr Social Media Posts

## LinkedIn

I built an app I've wanted for years.

Whenever I have a concert coming up, I always do the same thing: go to setlist.fm, look at what the band has been playing lately, then manually build a playlist in Apple Music. Song by song. It's tedious.

So I built SetListr.

It pulls from setlist.fm and connects to Apple Music. Browse any artist's concert history, see full setlists, and create playlists with one tap. The killer feature: it generates "concert prep" playlists based on what a band has actually been playing recently — so you know what you're likely to hear live.

I know it's a niche app. I built it mostly for me. But if you're the kind of person who likes to know the setlist before the lights go down, it might be for you too.

It's in beta on TestFlight now. Sign up at setlistr.app

If you're a Spotify user and interested, let me know — it's on the roadmap.

Full write-up on my blog: https://mikenoe.com/posts/setlistr/
